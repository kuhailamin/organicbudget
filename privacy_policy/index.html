Security

Organic Budget does not take any personal identifying bank and credit card account information as our Service does not sync with financial institutions. Data is inserted manually into the Site. This is a high-powered security measure we have taken to protect you and your data. To ensure SSL is enabled on your computer, you will see the URL prefix "HTTPS" in your browser.
